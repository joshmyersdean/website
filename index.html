Research

Research Interests

The goal of my research is to develop computer vision, machine learning, and graphics techniques to understand, analyze, and visualize how the world changes over time. Much of the current work in my research group focuses on two ongoing lines of work:

  • Analyzing and identifying unusual content in video streams from live-streaming webcams.
  • Using per-pixel features from deep neural networks to improve lower-level vision and image processing tasks like image segmentation, seam carving, and graph cuts.
